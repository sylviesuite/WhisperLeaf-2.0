# WhisperLeaf Manifesto

## AI that belongs to you

We believe artificial intelligence should serve people.

Not platforms.
Not advertising systems.
Not endless subscriptions.

WhisperLeaf is built on a simple idea:

AI should belong to the individual.

---

## Principles

### 1. Human First

AI exists to support human thinking, creativity, and decision-making.

It should never replace human agency or control.

---

### 2. Ownership

Users should own their AI tools.

No forced subscriptions.
No rented intelligence.

---

### 3. Local by Default

WhisperLeaf runs locally whenever possible.

Your data stays on your machine.

---

### 4. Privacy is a Foundation

Your thoughts, documents, and conversations belong to you.

They should not be harvested, sold, or analyzed by distant servers.

---

### 5. Calm Technology

AI should reduce noise, not create it.

WhisperLeaf is designed to support thoughtful work rather than addictive engagement.

---

### 6. Sustainable Intelligence

Efficient local AI can reduce reliance on large centralized data centers.

We believe the future of AI should be both powerful and responsible.

---

## The Goal

A future where people have their own intelligence tools.

Personal. Private. Sovereign.

AI that belongs to you.
