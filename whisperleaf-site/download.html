What to expect

  1. 1

    Click the button and wait for the file to save

    The installer is about 29 MB — it should download in under a minute on most connections. Your browser will save it to your Downloads folder.

  2. 2

    Windows may warn you about the file

    After downloading, you may see a warning at the bottom of your browser. Click the small arrow next to "Delete" and choose "Keep anyway".

    This is normal for new software that hasn't been downloaded by millions of people yet.
  3. 3

    A second security screen may appear

    When you double-click the installer, Windows SmartScreen may appear. Click "More options" (or "More info"), then "Run anyway".

    WhisperLeaf is safe — this warning appears because the app is new and not yet widely recognised by Windows.
  4. 4

    The setup window opens and does everything for you

    A progress window will appear and handle the rest — installing the AI, setting everything up, and opening WhisperLeaf in your browser when it's ready. No technical knowledge needed.
