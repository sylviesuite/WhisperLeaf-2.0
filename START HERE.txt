Welcome to WhisperLeaf Beta!
============================

Getting started is easy:

  1. Extract this zip folder somewhere on your computer
     (right-click → "Extract All..." works great)

  2. Open the extracted "whisperleaf-beta" folder

  3. Double-click "Start WhisperLeaf.bat" to launch

  4. Optional: Right-click "Start WhisperLeaf.bat" → Send to → Desktop
     (create shortcut) for quick access next time.

That's it! WhisperLeaf will open in your browser automatically.
Everything runs locally — nothing leaves your machine.
