Why WhisperLeaf

Most AI tools live far away on remote servers. Your documents, notes, and ideas have to travel just to get help.

WhisperLeaf runs on your own device. It reads and reasons locally, so your work stays where it belongs—with you.

Safety & Privacy

Private by default. Safe by design.

All processing happens locally on your device. Your data is not sent to external servers, and nothing is stored or used without your explicit control.

Memory is fully user-controlled. Content is only saved when you choose, and context is isolated to prevent unintended carryover between conversations.

This approach reduces common risks associated with cloud-based AI systems, including data leakage, unintended retention, and cross-context contamination.

WhisperLeaf is designed in alignment with established safety and risk management principles, including:

  • OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications
  • NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF)
  • GDPR and privacy-by-design standards

While WhisperLeaf is not a compliance-certified system, its local-first architecture and explicit memory controls make it well-suited for privacy-sensitive workflows.

Unlike cloud-based AI tools, WhisperLeaf keeps your data on your device and under your control at all times.

There are no external calls, no background indexing, and no hidden memory.
