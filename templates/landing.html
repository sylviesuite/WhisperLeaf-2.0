Why WhisperLeaf

Most AI tools live far away on remote servers. Your documents, notes, and ideas have to travel just to get help.

WhisperLeaf runs on your own device. It reads and reasons locally, so your work stays where it belongs—with you.

How it works

Download

Install WhisperLeaf on your computer. No account or signup flow required.

Setup

Choose a local model, point WhisperLeaf at your documents, and confirm your preferences.

Use

Chat, summarize, and explore ideas in a calm workspace that never sends your writing away.

See what your AI is doing

WhisperLeaf makes local processing visible. You can see when it’s working, which documents it’s using, and what stays on-device.

  • Understand when and how WhisperLeaf is using your content.
  • Verify that no ordinary workflows send data to remote servers.
  • Review benchmarks that come from your own machine.
