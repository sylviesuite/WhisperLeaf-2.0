Why WhisperLeaf

Most AI tools live far away on remote servers. Your documents, notes, and ideas have to travel just to get help.

WhisperLeaf runs on your own device. It reads and reasons locally, so your work stays where it belongs—with you.

How it works

Bring your work in

Drop in a document, paste a note, or start with a private question. WhisperLeaf gives you a local workspace for thinking, writing, and analysis.

Process locally

WhisperLeaf runs on your device, so your content stays with you while the model reads, summarizes, and helps you work through ideas.

Choose what stays

Save useful context only when you want to. Memory is explicit, reviewable, and under your control.

A private workflow from input to insight — without sending your work to the cloud.

Safety & Privacy

Private by default. Safe by design.

WhisperLeaf processes your work locally on your device. Your data is not sent to external servers during normal use, and nothing is stored unless you choose it.

Memory is user-controlled. Saved context should be explicit, reviewable, and isolated to reduce unintended carryover between conversations.

WhisperLeaf is designed around local-first, privacy-respecting principles for people working with sensitive ideas, drafts, and documents.

No external calls. No background indexing. No hidden memory.

Future Direction
